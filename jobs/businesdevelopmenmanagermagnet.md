About Us
We are a growth company operating in Web3, currently working with a Web3 protocol in the DeFi landscape. The protocol is building financial infrastructure and solutions for blockchain-based businesses, delivering high-impact, B2B financial services across the crypto ecosystem.

As we continue scaling, we’re looking for a highly connected and execution-driven Business Development Manager to accelerate growth and expand our network.

The Role
We are seeking a Web3-native BD professional with a strong existing network across DeFi and the broader crypto ecosystem. This is a high-impact, revenue-focused role with two primary growth channels:

Direct Business Development – sourcing and closing projects directly into the protocol.
Partner-Led Growth – building and managing a network of strategic partners who can drive deal flow to us.
You’ll be responsible for originating opportunities, running demos, closing deals, and managing partner relationships — all while operating independently within a defined BD framework.

Key Responsibilities
Leverage your existing Web3 network to source and onboard new B2B clients.
Identify DeFi and crypto projects that can benefit from our financial solutions.
Run demo calls and present protocol solutions to founders, executives, and decision-makers.
Manage the full BD lifecycle: outreach, qualification and closing.
Build and maintain a partner network (agencies, service providers, ecosystems, etc.) that can refer and generate deal flow.
Manage and motivate partners to actively bring business to the protocol.
Track pipeline activity and performance within our BD system.
Provide market feedback and insights from conversations with projects.
What We’re Looking For
Proven experience in Web3 business development, preferably within DeFi.
An existing, active network of crypto founders, protocols, service providers, and ecosystem players.
Strong understanding of the DeFi landscape and crypto-native financial products.
Experience selling B2B solutions in Web3.
Comfortable leading demo calls and explaining technical/financial solutions clearly.
A self-starter and solo operator who can manage their own pipeline and deliver results.
Strong relationship-building and negotiation skills.
Results-driven with a clear track record of bringing in revenue or strategic partnerships.
Nice to Have
Experience working at a Web3 protocol or infrastructure provider.
Experience building or managing channel/partner programs.
Deep understanding of token ecosystems and crypto-native business models.
Why Join Us?
Fully remote role.
High ownership and autonomy.
Direct impact on protocol growth.
Competitive compensation (base + performance incentives).
Opportunity to work at the forefront of DeFi innovation.
